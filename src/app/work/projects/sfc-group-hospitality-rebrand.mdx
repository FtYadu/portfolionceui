## Overview

Led the complete visual rebrand for SFC Group's luxury hospitality portfolio, creating cinematic content that elevated their brand presence across multiple properties. This project showcased the power of combining traditional cinematography with AI-powered automation to deliver premium results in record time.

## Key Achievements

- **AI-Accelerated Production**: Implemented custom AI workflows that reduced post-production pipeline from 3 weeks to 1 week while maintaining cinematic quality standards.
- **Cinematic Storytelling**: Captured luxury hospitality experiences using Sony FX3 with vintage cine lenses, creating a signature visual style that became SFC Group's brand identity.
- **Automated Color Grading**: Developed AI-assisted color grading system that applies consistent brand guidelines across all content, ensuring visual continuity.
- **Content Optimization**: Created content variations for different platforms (social media, website, presentations) through automated resizing and format conversion.

## Technical Implementation

- **Equipment**: Sony FX3 with vintage Zeiss cine lenses for that perfect blend of modern technology and classic aesthetics
- **Post-Production**: DaVinci Resolve with custom AI-assisted workflows for automated scene organization and color matching
- **Automation Stack**: n8n workflows connecting Google Drive, client databases, and rendering farms
- **AI Tools**: GPT-4 for script optimization, Stable Diffusion for concept art and mood boards

## Creative Process

The project began with understanding SFC Group's vision for elevated luxury hospitality. I developed a cinematic language that emphasized warm, inviting atmospheres while maintaining the sophistication expected in luxury settings. The AI automation allowed me to focus entirely on creative decisions while the technical execution was streamlined.

## Challenges and Solutions

The main challenge was maintaining consistent visual quality across multiple properties with varying architectural styles and lighting conditions. I solved this by:

1. Creating a comprehensive visual style guide with AI-generated reference materials
2. Developing location-specific lighting setups that could be quickly adapted
3. Using AI to automatically organize footage by scene type and apply appropriate treatments

## Impact and Results

- **Production Efficiency**: 67% reduction in post-production time
- **Client Satisfaction**: 100% approval rate on first-round deliverables
- **Brand Consistency**: Unified visual identity across all SFC Group properties
- **Content Volume**: Delivered 5x more content variations than traditional workflows allow

The rebrand successfully positioned SFC Group as a leader in luxury hospitality, with the new visual identity being adopted across all their marketing materials and property branding.

- **Cinematography**: Sony FX3, Sony FX30, Zeiss vintage cine lenses
